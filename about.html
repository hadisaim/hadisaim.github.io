About Us

With today’s ever growing application of advanced technologies on all fields the reality of true quality and system reliability has become one of the most valuable factor in the planning and design as well as in the construction. Our activities are tailored to meet the individual needs of our clients with the support & IFE of strong team providing professional & high quality services. IFE is intrinsically sensitive to the needs of its clients & to the requirements of an ever changing technological world. We strive to consistently deliver superior services to our clients, by having an ideal mix of performance, experience and quality, by hiring the best people and following best global practices. Quality is the drive behind our business with a Customer Focused approach. All our Business processes are developed, implemented and continually improved with a clear goal of achieving and exceeding client’s satisfaction and demonstrating continual improvement in all our key processes.

Company Summary

Integrated Field Establishment is a full service Contracting Establishment that can be counted on to provide services including engineering, design, procurement, project management, construction and Construction management, environmental consulting, management consulting, quality assurance and quality control, information management, operations and maintenance, and process technology development to provide complete contracting services to residential, commercial, and industrial customers.

Company History

Since 2006, Integrated Field Establishment has provided construction and energy solutions to home and business owners. With a focus on general contracting Integrated field Establishment provides a full range of services and can meet virtually any construction need. No project is too small or too large. Every project adds value to a property and increases security, comfort and convenience.

Integrated Field Establishment prides themselves in its ability to meet the latest innovative designs and concepts. The establishment's combined ten years of skills, knowledge, available resources and a reputation for customer satisfaction makes Integrated Field Establishment the preferred support to contractors.
