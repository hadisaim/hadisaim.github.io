HSE CONSULTANCY

Occupational Health and Safety Environment has become an essential part of every industry. Without proper HSE Management and Safety Management System, no industry can flourish.
We provide High Quality HSE Services to meet the dramatically increase in the HSE Management and services demand in Industries worldwide. We are focused on Safe Execution of the projects, meeting the requirements of our Clients.
We have a competitive team comprised of highly professional and experienced Personals. Our team is qualified for making Safety documents and Plans, as per standards of the clients such as Saudi Aramco, SABIC and Royale Commission. We provide our clients with following project specific documents, comprised in accordance with Scope of work:


Method Statement     
Hazard Identification Plan
Job Safety Analysis
Environment Protection Plan
Waste Management Plan
Traffic Management Plan
Emergency Response Plan
Heat Stress Plan

In addition to above documentation, we provide a complete Safety Management System (SMS), with daily inspection routines, checklists and warning letter system, to ensure Site safety and minimize Unsafe acts. Our site safety officers conduct safety training on daily and weekly basis, to train the workers for safe execution of Job.

Our goal is to safely execute the projects awarded to us by our Clients, with Zero Accidents, recording maximum Safe working Hours without LTI.
